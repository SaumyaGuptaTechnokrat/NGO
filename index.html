    About SaveChildren

    SaveChildren is an NGO based in Ayodhya, dedicated to improving the lives of children in need. Our mission is to provide education, healthcare, and support to children who are facing poverty, hunger, and lack of opportunities. With a team of dedicated professionals and volunteers, we work tirelessly to create a positive impact in the lives of these children, helping them break free from the cycle of poverty and giving them a chance for a brighter future. Through our various programs and initiatives, we aim to empower children and give them the tools they need to thrive and succeed.
